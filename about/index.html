About

January 12, 2018

I am currently working as a Senior Security Tester at one of the biggest software vendors in the world. I started my career after graduating from university as a Developer, doing some low level networking and configuration stuff in the kernel, where I developed in C/C++, Java and some Python.

Most of my spare time I am spending on CTF’s and/or further education. CTF’s offer a great opportunity to educate one in this area in a short time. Besides getting practice, you also sharpen your mind and learn to look at things from a different angle.

Currently I am addicted to Hackthebox and vulnhub. As I am planning to get my certification in either CEH or OSCP by the end of the year, I have to practice a lot.

I will use this blog mainly to explain my thought processes in the CTF’s, as cheat sheet and some personal thoughts.

Enjoy reading!
